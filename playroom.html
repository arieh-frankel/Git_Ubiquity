Welcome to Playroom.

Playroom is a revolutionairy streamlined messaging application. Playroom was designed to make the life of day care workers easier. With a few simple one handed touches, child care workers are able to send complex messages. These messages can be chosen from a list of pre defined messages, and then sent directly to parents.
