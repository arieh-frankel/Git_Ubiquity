Welcome to Playroom.

Playroom is a revolutionairy streamlined messaging application. Playroom was designed to make the life of day care workers easier. With a few simple touches, child care workers are able to send complex messages. These messages can be chosen from pre defined list, and then sent directly to parents.

<

Designed for daycares

Through communicating directly with daycare workers, the Ubiquity design team have been able to implement and refine the Playroom messaging service. Parents will be notified, and will have the opportunity to direclt respond.
